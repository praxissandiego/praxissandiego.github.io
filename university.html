Praxis Community University

← to Praxis SD

We want to have a place where people can teach and learn from each other for fun in a setting outside of traditional academia —— a community university —— so we have started one! Our goal is to cultivate an environment where learning is enjoyable, by-the-community and for-the-community, and as widely accessible as possible. We are inspired by learning places like Fractal U NYC, Strother School of Radical Attention, Shoshin College, AntiUniversity Now and other similar efforts across the world.

You can browse all the past classes offered at Praxis CU in our class archive.
