Course Description

This is a class specifically designed for all the adults who were never taught how to cook and feel too overwhelmed to know where to start learning. You will learn about food and knife safety, how to prepare and cook a variety of dishes, as well as how to grocery shop effectively for your diet and budget.

Anybody of any experience level can join, but if you already feel very comfortable with cooking you might not gain as much. This class is designed for people who have not yet had the opportunity to learn many of the basics and feel overwhelmed in their kitchen, to get them comfortable and give them more agency. If you are looking to join the class but are worried about asking “stupid questions,” or that you’ll make mistakes with cutting an onion, this is exactly the class for you! There are no stupid questions here, and all mistakes are just an opportunity to learn.

All class materials and ingredients will be provided. The class is FREE to all those who need it, and pay-what-you-can to those who can afford it, just to help cover cost of ingredients!

About Your Instructor

I am both self-taught and classically trained and have worked in catering, a hotel, and as a line cook for the last several years. I am most familiar with French and Italian cooking but I value diversifying my skills and palate, and am able to work well with a variety of cuisines.
